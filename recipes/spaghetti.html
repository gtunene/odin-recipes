Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked spaghetti with the sauce. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
